About Me

Born in the small town of Dologon Maramag Bukidnon Philippines on November 14, 1994. Felmore is the son of the farmer Fernando R. Flores and his wife Maricel L. Flores. Fernando, a hardworking man, raised his four children in a very disciplined way. It was from here that Felmore learned much of his punctuality, adaptability, and spirituality.

He can play the piano, flute, harmonica, guitar, ukulele, and violin. He loves music pretty much.

He serves his mission in Angeles Mission Philippines in 2015-2017. He got married to a beautiful woman Leah in Manila Temple on February 28, 2020, and both have 2 kids Aelle and Uri. Currently, he is working full-time as Marketing Insights Officer at Cloudstaff. He is taking a Bachelor of Science in Applied Technology. He loves computers and really wants to be a Software Engineer in the future. He currently serves as a ward clerk in Maramag Ward, Malaybalay District.
